Hiii, I'm TOKALO Bi Tokalo Armel

I am currently a first-year engineering student in Industrial Management at Mohammed VI Polytechnic University (EMINES). My academic journey began at the Scientific High School of Yamoussoukro and continued through preparatory classes at the Polytechnic University of San Pedro, reflecting my perseverance and love for intellectual challenges.

Passionate about innovation, I am deeply interested in two rapidly evolving fields: Data Science and Aviation. Emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence fascinate me, and I am determined to play an active role in shaping their future.

My journey from Côte d’Ivoire to Morocco has been full of challenges. Each obstacle has taught me to be more resilient, better organized, and open to learning from failure. These experiences have shaped a strong sense of critical thinking and adaptability.

Outside of academics, I enjoy swimming and am an avid football enthusiast. I deeply value teamwork, discipline, and the constant pursuit of self-improvement.

Today, I aim to be a proactive contributor in ambitious projects that combine technology, operational excellence, and sustainable innovation.
